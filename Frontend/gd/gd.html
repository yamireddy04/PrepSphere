What is a GD?

A structured discussion to evaluate communication, critical thinking, and teamwork.

Introduction Body Conclusion

Skills Assessed

  • • Communication
  • • Teamwork
  • • Problem-solving
  • • Leadership
  • • Flexibility
  • • Subject knowledge
  • • Listening skills

🔹 Sample GD Topics

▶ Current Affairs
  • Is Artificial Intelligence a threat to jobs?
  • India’s stance on climate change: Is it enough?
  • Impact of Social Media on Politics
  • Is the Indian economy recovering post-COVID?
  • One Nation, One Election – Pros and Cons
▶ Abstract Topics
  • Red is the color of power
  • Numbers speak louder than words
  • When money speaks, truth is silent
  • Success is a journey, not a destination
  • Freedom vs. Discipline
▶ Business & Economy
  • Should cryptocurrencies be legalized in India?
  • Is privatization the only way forward for PSUs?
  • Startup culture in India – Sustainable or a bubble?
  • Gig economy: Empowering or Exploitative?
  • Are mergers and acquisitions good for the economy?
▶ Social Issues
  • Is reservation system still relevant in India?
  • Gender equality: How far have we come?
  • The rise of fake news – Who is responsible?
  • Online education vs. traditional education
  • Mental health: Still a taboo in India?
▶ Technology
  • Is data the new oil?
  • Can India become a global tech leader?
  • Impact of 5G on digital India
  • Should there be limits on AI development?
  • Tech addiction among youth – A growing concern

Tips for Effective Participation

Prepare and research topics
