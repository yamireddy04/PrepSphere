Aptitude

Aptitude refers to one's natural ability or talent.

Why Used in Job Assessments:

  • Predicts job performance
  • Fair and objective
  • Cost-effective
  • Standardized
  • Focuses on potential

Types of Aptitude Tests:

  • Verbal
  • Numerical
  • Logical
  • Spatial
