2. Direct Messaging Recruiters on LinkedIn

What It Is:
Direct messaging on LinkedIn involves sending short, personalized messages to HRs or recruiters to initiate a conversation about job openings or opportunities.

When to Use:

  • After seeing a job posting on LinkedIn
  • To express interest in a specific team or role
  • To follow up on a submitted application

Best Practices:

  • Keep it concise and professional
  • Avoid sending your resume immediately unless asked
  • Always personalize (mention their role, company, or a recent post)
Sample LinkedIn Message 
Hi [Recruiters Name],

I hope you're doing well. I'm [Name], [education], and I am interested in [type] roles at [Company Name]. I recently came across your profile and wanted to connect to learn more about openings in your team.

I have worked on projects involving Angular, Python, and AI-based tools. I am confident my skills in full-stack development and my passion for creating efficient solutions would be a great asset.

Looking forward to connecting!

Best,
