Non-Technical Career Roadmaps

Structured blueprints for professional business and creative roles

01 📈 Marketing & Communications Roadmap

Goal: Develop and execute marketing strategies, manage brand image.

  • Digital Marketing: SEO, SEM, Social Media Marketing
  • Content Creation: Copywriting, Blogging, Video Production
  • Public Relations: Media Outreach, Crisis Communication
  • Market Research: Consumer Behavior, Trend Analysis
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Campaign Performance
  • Tools: HubSpot, Mailchimp, CRM Software
  • Portfolio: Build case studies of successful campaigns
  • Path: Marketing Coordinator → Marketing Specialist → Marketing Manager
02 🧑‍🤝‍ Human Resources (HR) Roadmap

Goal: Manage employee relations, talent acquisition, and organizational development.

  • HR Fundamentals: Recruitment, Onboarding, Compensation & Benefits
  • Labor Law & Compliance: EEO, OSHA, FMLA
  • HRIS: Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, ADP
  • Employee Relations: Conflict Resolution, Performance Management
  • Talent Management: Training & Development, Succession Planning
  • Certifications: SHRM-CP/SCP, HRCI (PHR/SPHR)
  • Experience: HR Intern → HR Generalist → HR Manager
03 💰 Finance & Accounting Roadmap

Goal: Manage financial records, investments, or provide financial advice.

  • Accounting Principles: GAAP, IFRS
  • Financial Modeling & Analysis: Valuation, Forecasting
  • Software Proficiency: Excel (advanced), QuickBooks, SAP ERP
  • Certifications: CPA (Certified Public Accountant), CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst)
  • Investment Knowledge: Stocks, Bonds, Mutual Funds, Derivatives
  • Risk Management: Credit Risk, Market Risk
  • Ethical Standards: Fiduciary Duty, Regulatory Compliance
  • Path: Junior Accountant → Financial Analyst → Finance Manager
04 🤝 Sales & Business Development Roadmap

Goal: Drive revenue by acquiring new clients and growing existing accounts.

  • Sales Techniques: Prospecting, Cold Calling, Presentation Skills
  • Communication & Negotiation: Active Listening, Persuasion
  • CRM Software: Salesforce, Zoho CRM, Microsoft Dynamics
  • Lead Generation: Networking, Digital Outreach
  • Product Knowledge: Deep understanding of offerings
  • Target Setting & Forecasting: Sales Metrics, Quotas
  • Relationship Building: Client Retention, Upselling
  • Path: Sales Development Rep → Account Executive → Sales Manager
05 🗓️ General Project Management Roadmap

Goal: Lead and execute projects efficiently across various industries.

  • Project Lifecycle: Initiation, Planning, Execution, Monitoring, Closing
  • Methodologies: Agile, Scrum, Waterfall, Hybrid
  • Tools: Jira, Asana, Monday.com, Microsoft Project
  • Risk Management: Identification, Mitigation, Contingency Planning
  • Stakeholder Communication: Reporting, Expectation Management
  • Leadership & Team Building: Delegation, Motivation
  • Certifications: PMP, CAPM, CSM (Certified ScrumMaster)
  • Path: Project Coordinator → Project Manager → Senior Project Manager
06 🧑‍🏫 Education & Teaching Roadmap

Goal: Educate and inspire students in various subjects and settings.

  • Subject Matter Expertise: Deep knowledge in chosen field
  • Pedagogy & Didactics: Teaching Theories, Learning Styles
  • Classroom Management: Behavior, Engagement Strategies
  • Curriculum Development: Lesson Planning, Assessment Design
  • Certifications: State Teaching License, Subject-specific endorsements
  • Student Assessment: Formative, Summative, Differentiated Instruction
  • Professional Development: Ongoing Learning, Collaboration
  • Path: Student Teacher → Classroom Teacher → Department Head / Administrator
07 🏥 Healthcare Administration Roadmap

Goal: Manage operations in hospitals, clinics, or public health organizations.

  • Healthcare Systems: Knowledge of policies, regulations (HIPAA)
  • Medical Terminology: Understanding clinical language
  • Financial Management: Budgeting, Billing, Revenue Cycle
  • Leadership & Operations: Staffing, Quality Improvement
  • EHR Systems: Epic, Cerner, Meditech
  • Public Health Concepts: Epidemiology, Health Policy
  • Compliance: Accreditation Standards, Legal Issues
  • Path: Administrative Assistant → Healthcare Coordinator → Clinic Manager
08 ✍️ Journalism & Content Writing Roadmap

Goal: Research, write, and report news or create engaging content for various platforms.

  • Research Skills: Fact-checking, Source Verification
  • Interviewing Techniques: Asking effective questions
  • Writing & Editing: Clarity, Conciseness, Storytelling
  • Media Ethics & Law: Defamation, Copyright
  • SEO & Digital Content: Keywords, Web Analytics
  • CMS Platforms: WordPress, Joomla, Squarespace
  • Multimedia Skills: Basic video/audio editing (optional)
  • Portfolio: Showcase articles, blog posts, scripts
  • Path: Intern/Freelancer → Staff Writer → Editor / Senior Journalist
09 🧑‍⚖️ Legal Professions Roadmap

Goal: Provide legal advice, represent clients, or conduct legal research.

  • Legal Education: Bachelor's Degree → Law School (J.D.)
  • Bar Examination: State-specific licensing exam
  • Legal Research: Westlaw, LexisNexis, Caselaw
  • Specialized Law: Corporate, Criminal, Environmental, Family, IP Law
  • Moot Court & Debating: Argumentation Skills
  • Client Counseling: Interviewing, Advising
  • Ethics: Professional Conduct, Confidentiality
  • Path: Law Clerk → Associate Attorney → Partner / Senior Counsel
10 🎭 Creative Arts & Design Roadmap (Non-Digital)

Goal: Express creativity through various artistic mediums or design spaces.

  • Foundational Skills: Drawing, Painting, Sculpting, Photography
  • Art History & Theory: Context, Movements, Critiques
  • Medium Specialization: Ceramics, Textiles, Printmaking, Interior Design, Fashion Design
  • Portfolio Development: Curated collection of best works
  • Business Skills: Pricing, Marketing (for freelancers/artists)
  • Networking: Exhibitions, Galleries, Design Fairs
  • Critique & Iteration: Receiving and applying feedback
  • Path: Emerging Artist/Designer → Established Professional → Educator / Curator
