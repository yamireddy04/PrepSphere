Conventional Roadmap

A standard operating procedure for preparing for interviews is:

Foundation Building

1. Choose a Path, something that you truly want to become, achieve and willing to put your blood into.

2. Prepare your resume (acc to field chosen)

3. Prepare your Cover Letter (acc to field chosen)

Preparation

Prepare for assessments (interviews)

Mock Interviews: Practice mock questions of all interview rounds (PrepMaster, MockIt, MockMyInterview)

Apply for Jobs

Use HireHub to find job registrations with details

Post-Interview Reflection: Reflect on the interview process and identify areas for improvement.
